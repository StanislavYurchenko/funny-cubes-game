Rules
Welcome to Funny cubes game!
  • The goal of the game is to score as many points as possible
  • You get 2 points for the green die
  • You get 1 point for the yellow die
  • You will lose 1 point per red die
  • When you click on a green, yellow or red cube, one, two or zero new cubes may appear
  • The game ends after 1 minute or if all the spaces are filled with colored cubes or colored cubes disappear completely

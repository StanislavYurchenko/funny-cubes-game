Rules
Welcome to Funny square game!
  • The goal of the game is to score as many points as possible
  • You get 2 points for the green square
  • You get 1 point for the yellow square
  • You will lose 1 point per red square
  • When you click on a green, yellow or red square, one, two or zero new cubes may appear
  • The game ends after time is left or if all the spaces are filled with colored squares or colored squares disappear completely
